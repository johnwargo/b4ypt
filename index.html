Salaries & Benefits for YMCA Personal Trainers

The Personal Training staff at YMCA of Greater Charlotte facilities, even though they're highly trained, experienced, and certified professionals, aren't employees. Because of this, they don't have regular hours, vacation or medical benefits. Many of these trainers struggle to provide for their families because they simply can't maintain a consistent income and therefore cannot afford benefits.

We're asking for your help to push YMCA leadership to change their position on this, and do the right thing for these dedicated employees.
